## üìä EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Derrimut 24:7 Gym** is one of Australia's largest fitness chains, founded in 2010 by Nick Solomos. The brand operates **18+ locations** across Victoria and South Australia, with plans to expand to 300 gyms globally by 2030. The gym chain is known for its **24/7 access**, state-of-the-art equipment, diverse group classes, and integrated supplement and cafe services.

**Key Characteristics:**
- **Brand Identity:** "Believe in Yourself" tagline
- **Business Model:** Subscription-based with upfront and direct-debit options
- **Pricing:** AUD $14.95-$19.95 per fortnight (varies by plan)
- **Locations:** Primarily Victoria (VIC) and South Australia (SA)
- **Services:** 24/7 access, group fitness, personal training, supplements, sauna, Pilates
